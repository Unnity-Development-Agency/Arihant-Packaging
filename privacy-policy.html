2 What Data We Collect

We collect only the following personal data, and only when you voluntarily submit it through our enquiry form:

Data Collected Why We Collect It
Full Name To address you in our response
Email Address To send you a reply to your enquiry
Phone Number (if provided) To follow up, if required
Message / Enquiry Content To understand and respond to your business requirement
Enquiry Category To route your enquiry to the right team

We do not collect any sensitive personal data such as financial information, government IDs, or health data.

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your business enquiry via email or phone.
  • To send product catalogues or pricing information you have requested.
  • To maintain a record of our business correspondence with you.

We will never use your data for unsolicited marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making without your explicit consent.

4 How We Store Your Data

Enquiries submitted through the Website are received directly into our business email inbox (arihantpackagingdelhi@gmail.com). We do not operate a proprietary database of personal information.

We retain enquiry data for a reasonable business period to support ongoing correspondence, and delete it when it is no longer needed.

5 Sharing of Data

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Where required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Arihant Packaging or others.

6 Cookies

Our website may use basic browser cookies or analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect your ability to use the Website.

7 Your Rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, you have the following rights as a data principal:

Right to Access

Request details of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data from our records.

Right to Grievance

Raise a complaint with our team and receive a timely response.

To exercise any of the above rights, write to us at arihantpackagingdelhi@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
