Rooted in Delhi, Growing India-wide.

Arihant Packaging is a premier manufacturer and trader specializing in high-quality, eco-conscious disposable paper products. With our head office situated in Gautam Nagar, New Delhi, and a state-of-the-art manufacturing unit in Karnal, Haryana, we are strategically positioned to serve businesses of all scales.

We operate through two dedicated verticals: providing robust B2B raw material supply to manufacturers across the region, and producing our own line of finished, ready-to-use premium products under the DIVAYU brand.
